About

Ryan Weald is a data scientist at Sharethrough where he uses data to rid the internet of disruptive advertising. He studied computer science and bioinformatics at UCSD before dropping out to try and make his mark on the world. In his spare time Ryan contributes to open source, hacks on healthcare, and dodges sharks while surfing in Northern California
